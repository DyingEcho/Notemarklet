Welcome to Notemarklet!
Notemarklet is a free, offline-capable note-taking bookmarklet.
Use it here, or install it by dragging this link to your bookmarks bar.
And the best part? It's completely free, so you can modify it to your heart's content! You don't even need to credit me (but it would be nice...)
